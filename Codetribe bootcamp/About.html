About Us

MS logistics is an African focused provider of integrated market access and logistics solutions. With a focus on the following key industries - healthcare, consumer, automotive, chemicals, industrial and commodities - we take our clients’ products to some of the fastest growing and most challenging markets in the world.

our purpose Everyone who works for Imperial is united by one, clearly defined common purpose - connecting Africa and the world and improving people’s lives with access to quality products and services. This means that - as a business and as individuals - we make a meaningful difference in people’s lives.
